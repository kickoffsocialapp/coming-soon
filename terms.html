6. Description of Services

KickOff provides the following services:

  • Match Organisation: Bookers can create matches by specifying the date, time, venue, number of players, and cost per player. The App generates balanced teams based on player ratings.
  • Match Discovery: Users can browse and join available matches in their area, subject to availability and payment.
  • Player Ratings: The App calculates and maintains player ratings based on match performance, used to generate fair team selections.
  • Groups: Users can create or join Groups to organise regular games with the same players.
  • In-App Messaging: Users can communicate with other players and Group members through the App's chat functionality.
  • Health Data Integration: With explicit user consent, the App can read health and fitness data (distance, heart rate, calories) from Apple HealthKit during matches.

8. Refunds, Cancellations, and Credits

Football matches organised through KickOff are leisure services provided on a specific, fixed date. Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013 (Regulation 28(1)(h)), services related to leisure activities provided on a specific date are exempt from the 14-day cooling-off period that ordinarily applies to distance contracts. How a payment is returned depends on the reason for the cancellation:

Refund to your original payment method. Where a paid game does not take place through no fault of yours, the amount you paid is refunded to the original payment method used. This applies when: the organiser cancels the game; the organiser fails to start the game or does not attend; the game is automatically cancelled by the App; or the organiser materially changes the time, price, or team size of a Discover game and you do not re-confirm your place. Such refunds are processed through Stripe and may take several days to reach your account, depending on your bank or card provider.

Refund to in-app Credits. Where you voluntarily withdraw from a game that still goes ahead, any amount you paid is returned to your in-app Credit balance (denominated in GBP), which can be applied to any future game. Credits are not returned to your original payment method. Credits do not expire while your account remains active.
