Code of Conduct

KickOff Social Football App
Terms Privacy Policy Code of Conduct
Last updated 3 July 2026

KickOff exists to help people play more football. Whether you're organising a game, joining one nearby, or chatting with your group, we want KickOff to feel welcoming, fair, and safe for everyone. This Code of Conduct sets out what we expect from every member of the community.

1. Play Fair and Be Respectful

Treat other players the way you'd want to be treated on the pitch. Be respectful in your messages, your username, and your behaviour at matches. Banter is part of football, but abuse, threats, harassment, discrimination, and hate speech of any kind are never acceptable.

2. Be Honest

Use your real identity and don't impersonate other people, public figures, or brands — including through your username. Don't manipulate player ratings, match scores, or any other data. Turn up when you say you will, and settle payments fairly.

3. Show Up and Communicate

Organisers and players rely on each other. If you can no longer make a match, let people know as early as possible so your spot can be filled. Organisers should run games as advertised and handle payments and refunds fairly through the App's Credits system.

4. Keep People Safe

Play within your ability, respect the venue and its rules, and look out for others. KickOff facilitates matches but is not responsible for what happens on the pitch — participate at your own risk, make sure you're fit to play, and consider appropriate insurance.

5. Respect Privacy

Don't share other players' personal information, photos, or messages outside the App without their consent. Health and fitness data is personal — it belongs to the individual player.

6. Reporting Problems

If someone is behaving badly, impersonating another person, or making you feel unsafe, please report it to us at kickoff.contactme@gmail.com. We take reports seriously and review them promptly.

7. Consequences

Breaking this Code of Conduct can lead to warnings, removal from matches or groups, username changes, or suspension or termination of your account, depending on the seriousness of the behaviour. Serious or repeated breaches, and anything unlawful, may be reported to the relevant authorities. These measures sit alongside our Terms & Conditions.

8. Contact

Questions about this Code of Conduct? Contact us at kickoff.contactme@gmail.com.
