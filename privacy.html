KickOff Social Football App
Terms Privacy Policy Code of Conduct
Last updated 3 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kick Off App Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the KickOff app. We are committed to protecting your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Kick Off App Ltd (Company Number 17291462) is the data controller responsible for your personal data. Our registered office is Unit 18, Park Royal Business Centre, 9-17 Park Royal Road, London, NW10 7LQ. For any privacy questions, contact us at kickoff.contactme@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, chosen username, and profile details.
  • Skills questionnaire responses: your answers used to calculate your initial player rating.
  • Location data: your device location, used to discover nearby matches (only while you are using the App and with your permission).
  • Match and performance data: match history, teams, scores, player ratings, and statistics.
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe. We receive confirmation of payment status but never your full card details.
  • Health and fitness data (optional): distance, heart rate, and calories from Apple HealthKit, only with your explicit consent.
  • Usage and device data: basic technical information needed to operate and secure the App.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to create and manage your account, generate balanced teams and player ratings, show you nearby matches, process payments through Stripe, enable in-app messaging, provide customer support, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with our legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR: performance of a contract (to provide the App's services to you); consent (for optional health data and location access, which you can withdraw at any time); legitimate interests (to keep the App safe, prevent fraud, and improve our services); and legal obligation (where we are required to retain or disclose data by law).

5. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd.).
  • Supabase — authentication and account management.
  • MongoDB Atlas — secure database storage.
  • Railway — application hosting.
  • Apple HealthKit — optional health data integration (processed on your device).
  • Google Maps — venue location and mapping.

Some profile information — such as your name, username, and player rating — is visible to other Users as a necessary part of the App's social and match-organising features. Your email address, payment details, and health data are never shared with other Users.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain certain records (for example, transaction records) to comply with legal obligations.

7. Data Security

Your personal data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and is not stored on our servers. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access your data; to request correction of inaccurate data; to request erasure; to restrict or object to processing; to data portability; and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kickoff.contactme@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Children

KickOff is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by other appropriate means. The date below shows when this policy was last updated.

11. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at kickoff.contactme@gmail.com.
